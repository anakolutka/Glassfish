To Stop Service Assemblies

  1. In the tree view, click the JBI node and then the Service Assemblies node.

    A list of Service Assemblies is displayed.

  2. In the Developer profile, click the checkbox next to the Service Assembly you want to stop and select Stop from the Operations drop-down menu.

    The State column identifies that the Service Assembly is stopped.

  3. In the Clusters profile, click on the Service Assembly you want to stop.

    The Properties page for the selected Service Assembly is displayed.

  4. In the Targets table, click the checkbox next to the target on which you are stopping the Service Assembly.

  5. Click Stop.

    The Status column identifies that the Service Assembly is stopped. Stopping a Service Assembly places it in state between Start and Shutdown where the component is maintained in memory. Stopping a Service Assembly target also disables it. A Service Assembly is always stopped on the named target. If a target is not specified, the Service Assembly is stopped on the default server instance. You can specify the desired target by selecting the Manage Targets button in the Targets table.
