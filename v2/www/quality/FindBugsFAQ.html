FindBugs Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is this document about?


A: This document provides information on the email that is sent out after a nightly run of FindBugs tool against project Glassfish binaries.

Q: What is the daily FindBugs email?


A: The daily FindBugs email is sent to the people whose code change in project GlassFish, has generated a warning in FindBugs report, possibly due to some minor Java Programming Bug.
In the event that the person who had changed the code is no longer available, the email is sent to the module owner.

Q: Where can I get more information on project Glassfish, FindBugs?


A: Glassfish: https://glassfish.dev.java.net
FindBugs: http://findbugs.sourceforge.net

Q: I don't want daily emails for bugs I've introduced. What should I do?


A: Fix all the high priority issues :)

Q: I want to find out medium and low priority warnings in my area. What should I do?


A: You may run FindBugs on your code, and generate the warnings you'd like to see.

Q: Who should I contact for further information?


A: You may contact Jagadesh Munta or Aditya Dada for further information regarding the FindBugs nightly runs made on project Glassfish.
