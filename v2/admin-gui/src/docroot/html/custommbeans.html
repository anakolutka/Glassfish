Custom MBeans

This page displays custom MBeans that are deployed to the server. From this page, you can do the following operations on the custom MBeans:

  • View information about the Custom MBeans deployed on the server. To do this, from the View drop-down list, select Detailed or Summary.

  • Deploy Custom MBeans. For steps to deploy a custom MBean, see Deploying and Enabling Custom MBeans.

  • Undeploy Custom MBeans. To undeploy a custom MBean, click the MBean you want to undeploy and click Undeploy.

  • Enable Custom MBeans. To enable a custom MBean, click the MBean you want to enable and click Enable.

  • Disable Custom MBeans. To disable a custom MBean, click the MBean you want to disable and click Disable.
