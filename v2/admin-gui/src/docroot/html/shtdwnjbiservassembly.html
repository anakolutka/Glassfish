To Disable or Shutdown Service Assemblies

  1. In the tree view, select the JBI node and then the Service Assemblies node.

    A list of Service Assemblies is displayed.

  2. In the Developer profile, click the checkbox next to all the Service Assemblies you want to shutdown and select Shut Down from the Operations drop-down menu.

    The State column identifies that the Service Assemblies are shutdown.

  3. In the Clusters profile, click the checkbox next to the Service Assemblies you want to disable.

    The Properties page for the selected Service Assembly is displayed.

  4. In the Targets table, click the checkbox next to all the Service Assemblies you are disabling.

  5. Click Disable.

    The Status column identifies that the Service Assemblies are disabled. You can specify the desired target by selecting the Manage Targets button in the Targets table.

  6. Alternatively, to start a single component on all targets, click the Service Assembly, select all the desired targets, then click the Shut Down button.
