The GlassFish Quality Matrix

The GlassFish community expressed a strong interest at JavaOne 2006 in the testing that goes behind GlassFish. We're happy to share with you all the details of the enormous breadth and depth of testing that happened before the final bits of GlassFish were delivered as Sun Java System Application Server Platform Edition 9.0!
