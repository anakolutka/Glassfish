Smoke/Pulse Tests

Testing Glassfish follows a streamlined process. We impose strict guidelines on both quality engineers as well as development engineers. Tests may be checked in but cannot be enabled for automated execution until they pass fixed criteria. Adherence to processes reduces headaches for everyone. But don't get intimidated - you may still develop and check-in a test. Please get in touch with us before enabling it, so that we can ensure it meets the requirements. This helps keep the testing going smoothly. GlassFish Pulse Tests
