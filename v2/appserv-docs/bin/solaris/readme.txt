To convert the manpages in appserv-docs repository:
cd to  appserv-docs/bin/solaris
and execute "convertSGML2Text".
This script will convert all the manpages in appserv-docs/sman/man1 to appserv-docs/sman/cat1

To convert the manpages in appserv-docs-ee repository:
cd  to appserv-docs/bin/solaris
and execute "convertSGML2Text  <location of appserv-docs-ee directory>"
