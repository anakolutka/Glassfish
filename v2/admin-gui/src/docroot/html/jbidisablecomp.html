To Disable a JBI Component

  1. In the tree view, click on the Integrations node and then the JBI Components node.

    A list of binding components and service engines appear in the JBI Components page. The Status column indicates whether a component is enabled or disabled.

  2. From the list, click the check box beside the required binding component or service engine and click Disable. The Status column indicates that the component is disabled.
