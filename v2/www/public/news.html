Keep up to date about activities in the GlassFish community! 

Since the GlassFish community is composed of a lot of technologies, many of which have their own project pages and announcement sections, we want to make it easier for you to find out what's going on. Plus, there are a lot of people involved in the GlassFish community, so we want to help you get in touch with them. You can use the list of news feeds and bloggers to keep up to date on the GlassFish community.

For highlights of what is happening on GlassFish see The Aquarium weblog.
