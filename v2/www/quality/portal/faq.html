How do I sign up?

It is very simple,after creating a java.net account, just email or fax a signed contributor agreement form and you are all set.To simply file an issue or participate in discussion forum, you don't need to sign contributor agreement form.

  1. Steps to become contributor
    1. Sign up for java.net userid. Default role is observer.
    2. Sign up SCA Contributor form
    3. Scan and Email this form to glassfish-qa at the rate of glassfish.dev.java.net or Fax at 408 404 5144
    4. Apply for Developer role by sending email to glassfish-qa at the rate of glassfish.dev.java.net
    5. Wait for confirmation email and start committing!
