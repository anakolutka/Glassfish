To View Metadata

  1. In the tree view, click on the JBI node, then the Service Assemblies node

  2. Click the Service Assemblies whose metadata you want to view.

    The properties page displays the details for the selected Service Assembly including its name, type, and metadata.

  3. Click the jbi.xml file.
