To Manage Service Assemblies in the Cluster Profile

The Service Assemblies page provides you information about the Service Assemblies currently deployed. It also enables you to deploy and undeploy Service Assemblies and also manage their lifecycle states.

  1. In the tree view, select the JBI node and then the Service Assemblies node.

  2. Use the State drop-down list to display Service Assemblies belonging to a desired state. For example, select Enabled to view all the Service Assemblies that are in the Enabled state.

  3. To deploy a new Service Assembly, click Deploy. For more steps on deployment, see the section ***link to the cluster prof task

  4. To enable a Service Assembly, click the check box next to the Service Assembly that you want to enable and click Enable.

  5. To disable a Service Assembly, click the check box next to the Service Assembly that you want to disable and click Disable.

  6. To delete a Service Assembly, click the check box next to the Service Assembly that you want to delete and click Delete.
