Processes

Testing is very methodical streamlined process.We impose stricter guidelines on ourselves compared to our developer engineers.No test can be checked in and enabled for automated execution until it passes some fixed criteria.Adherance to processes reduces headaches for everyone.But don't get intimated-you can still work in your own little world,check in the test but don't enable it until it satisfies all the criterias.This helps us to keep testing going. more»
